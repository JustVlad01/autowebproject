About Irish Motor Parts

Founded in 2016, Irish Motor Parts has been a leading provider of quality motor parts in County Sligo and beyond. Our mission is to supply our customers with the best parts at affordable prices, ensuring their vehicles stay in top condition.

Our History

Begun as a small family business, we have grown into a trusted name in the motor parts industry, thanks to our commitment to quality and customer service.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing not only the best products but also expert advice and excellent customer support. Our team works tirelessly to source parts that meet our high standards, ensuring satisfaction for every customer.

Why Choose Us?

With years of experience, a passion for motors, and a commitment to our customers, we stand out in our field. We offer free delivery on orders over 70 euros and are always ready to assist with any inquiries.
